## <p style='color:darkblue'>**Introduction**

---
[***<font style='color:red'>***NinjaTrader***</font>***](https://ninjatrader.com/) is a Fintech company  in the Investment Management & Fund Operators domain. It provides futures and forex trading and offers features such as market analytics, trade simulation and advanced charting, it also offers brokerage services, enabling users to use it for trading system development and trade simulation. 

***<font style='color:red'>***NinjaTrader*** </font>*** was recognized as the winner of two [<font style='color:orange'>Benzinga Global Fintech Awards</font>](https://ninjatrader.com/NewsReleases/NinjaTrader-Awarded-Best-Brokerage-for-Trading-Futures) in the annual event held November 10th, 2020, <font style='color:orange'>**The Trader Empowerment Award**</font> for its role in the growth of the futures trading community and <font style='color:orange'>**Best Brokerage for Trading Futures**</font> based on its unique combination of high-performance trading software, discount brokerage services and real-time support which is lowering the barrier for retail investors to enter the futures markets.

With its headquarter located in Denver, Colorado and Eliot Wickersheimer as the President, <font style='color:red'>***NinjaTrader***</font> has evolved into an industry leader supporting over 60,000 traders around the globe with best-in-class technology, discount commissions and world class support. <font style='color:red'><font style='color:red'>***NinjaTrader*** </font> </font>'s main competitors are MultiCharts, INVESTools and TradingView

## <p style='color:darkblue'>**Overview and Origin** 
---
Founded in 2003 by Raymon Deux, [***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>***](https://www.investopedia.com/ninjatrader-review-4706930) is an online trading software and market data service company that focuses on providing high-performance trading software  for active traders looking to trade stocks, options, futures and Forex products. With a funded account, the ***<font style='color:red'>***NinjaTrader***</font>*** platform is free to use for charting, market analysis, and live trading (the SIM version is free even without a funded account).

 The idea of the company came back to 2003 when Raymon Deux  found himself expecting more from his trading platform and, after a futile search for alternatives, he decided to build his own. By today this Company has not been funded.

### <p style='color:darkblue'>**Extra Info**

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** is building products and services which empower active traders to easily analyze and react to data from the world’s leading financial markets.

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** is intended for active traders. Actually it has a Market size: over 100,000 traders around the globe.

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** offers various free tools, low commissions and unlimited use of its free platform, making it a great option for new traders. 

* The brokerage’s greatest talking points are the ability to let traders simulate in a live market environment, a flexible interface, extensive data feed options and free demonstration options. It also offers a 2-week free trial when you sign up.

## <p align="left" width="100%"><font style='color:darkblue'>**Summarizing:** <font style='color:green'>**Pros** <font style='color:black'>& <font style='color:red'>**Cons** </font>

---

### <p style='color:green'>**Pros:**

* Futures trading on ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** is incredibly seamless. The company provides unlimited simulated futures trading, extensive historical data, unfiltered professional futures market data and a free 24-hour emergency trade desk.Including more than 100 indicators as well as access to third-party trading applications.

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** offers auto-trade algorithmic trading strategies across multiple future markets to eliminate emotional trading and save you time. It has the ability to place simulated trades, complete with an imaginary account and profit/loss summary.
 

* The ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>*** platform boasts excellent charting, real-time analysis, customizable technical indicators, Chart Trader (a chart-based order entry tool), and thousands of apps and add-ons from 3rd-party developers.


* “Quants,” are the brains behind complex trading programs. Specialists in this area guide securities firms, investment banks and hedge funds in making informed decisions about markets, pricing and financial risk.

* The platform supports all the usual order types, including market, limit, stop market, and stop-limit orders as well as advanced OCO (one cancels other) orders.

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>***'s ATM Strategies provide discretionary traders with semi-automated features to manage their positions. Alternatively, you can fully automate a strategy using a "point and click" construction for non-programmers, or by using ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>***'s C#-based trading framework.

### <p style='color:red'>**Cons:**

* It only support a setup for futures and forex traders.

* Investors looking for more extensive fundamental data research may have to look somewhere else.

* ***<font style='color:red'>NinjaTrader</font>***, for being a program you download and use locally, can create some challenges with execution speed of scans.  Particularly when involving hundreds of symbols.
